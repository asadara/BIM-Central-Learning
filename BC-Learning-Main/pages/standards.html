ISO 19650

ISO 19650 - Organization and Digitization of Information

Overview

Seri standar internasional untuk manajemen informasi konstruksi, yang mengatur framework untuk implementasi BIM secara efektif.

Key Standards

  • ISO 19650-1: Concepts and principles - ISO
  • ISO 19650-2: Delivery phase of assets - ISO
  • ISO 19650-3: Operational phase of assets - ISO
  • ISO 19650-4: Information containers - ISO
  • ISO 19650-5: Security-minded approach - ISO

Requirements

  • Common Data Environment (CDE)
  • Information management process
  • Information delivery planning
  • Master Information Delivery Plan (MIDP)
  • Audit trails and version control
BS 1192

BS 1192 - BIM Standards & PAS 1192

Overview

Seri standar BIM dari British Standards Institution yang menjadi dasar implementasi BIM di berbagai negara.

Key Standards

  • BS 1192-4: Collaborative production of info - BSI
  • PAS 1192-2: Construction info management - BSI
  • PAS 1192-3: Operational info management - BSI
  • PAS 1192-5: Security-minded approach - BSI
  • BS 8536: Briefing for design & construction - BSI

Collaboration Framework

  • Level of detail (LOD) specifications
  • Level of information (LOI) requirements
  • Work-in-progress (WIP) processes
  • Technical sanctions & reviews

IFC Standards - Industry Foundation Classes

Overview

Standar data terbuka untuk pertukaran informasi BIM antara aplikasi yang berbeda, dikelola oleh buildingSMART.

  • IFC 4.3: Latest GCC version (2024)

  • IFC 4.1: Extension release (2014)
  • IFC 4: Major update (2013)
  • IFC 2x3: koordinasi release
